Ad Blocker Chrome Extension

This Chrome extension provides a seamless and distraction-free browsing experience by blocking intrusive ads across various websites.

What it does:

  • Blocks banner ads and hides ad containers and sponsored content on web pages.
  • Improves page load times by preventing resource-heavy ads from loading.
  • Supports blocking ads from common ad networks such as Google Ad Services, DoubleClick, and more.

Enjoy faster and cleaner browsing with this ad-blocking solution!
